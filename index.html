Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo was always the easiest meal to make for my family that allowed me to "set it and forget it", ensuring them a warm, savory, filling meal.

Photo Credit: Nyonya Cooking

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons pork fat or avocado oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 lbs pork shoulder cut into large cubes/chunks
  • 2 teaspoons pepper corns
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup spiced vinegar or white vinegar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Set heat to medium/high and warm medium sized pot, add oil to warmed pot.
  2. Add garlic to pot and slightly brown whole garlic gloves in the hot oil.
  3. Add pork to pot and sear to get brown color on all sides.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients into pot and let come to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to medium/low and simmer for at least an hour.
  6. Remove the cover but continue simmering for another 30 minutes to reduce and thicken the sauce.
